Breaking Bad is my **all-time favorite series—nothing comes close in terms of character writing and storytelling.**

## Review

- The greatest character transformation in TV history (Walter White → Heisenberg)
- Every episode builds tension with almost zero filler
- Perfect balance of writing, acting, cinematography, and pacing

The story follows a struggling chemistry teacher who turns to making meth after a terminal cancer diagnosis, initially for his family—but slowly becomes consumed by power and ego . What makes it special is not just the plot, but how realistically it shows moral decay.

Every decision has consequences. Every action comes back later. The writing is so tight that even small details pay off seasons later.

The final season is simply **masterpiece-level**, delivering one of the most satisfying endings in television history—something very few shows achieve.
