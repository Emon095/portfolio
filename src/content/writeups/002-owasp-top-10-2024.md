A practical overview of OWASP priorities and how they impact real product teams.

## Priority themes

- Access control failures remain the most common root issue
- Secure defaults and policy checks should be centralized
- Input handling and logging quality directly affect detection speed

## Team playbook

Adopt threat modeling per feature and ship security checks inside CI, not as a final QA step.
